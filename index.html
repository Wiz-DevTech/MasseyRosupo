About the Organization

Strategic Holdings &
Asset Protection

As the administrative body of the CipherNex trust infrastructure, Massey & Rosupo Co. coordinates the intake, registration, and ledger assignment for all member subsidiary entities.

  • 01
    Ledger Document Registration
    Each filing entity submits its instrument here and receives a unique Document ID that is recorded on the CipherNex ledger — the foundation for CIPR issuance and reserve tracking.
  • 02
    UCC-1 Security Interests
    Perfected security interests filed with Delaware SOS, establishing priority lien positions over all collateral assets held by subsidiary entities.
  • 03
    Fiduciary Administration
    Independent fiduciary management separating operational liability from protected assets across the holding structure and wholly-owned subsidiaries.
  • 04
    Discharge & Settlement
    Upon CIPR burn/settlement, discharge evidence is generated with formal UCC 3-311 / 3-603 language and linked to the original registered document.

UCC Filing Authority & Discharge Protocol
All instruments registered through this portal create a perfected record on the CipherNex ledger, enabling downstream CIPR issuance, settlement, and formal discharge documentation.
  • ▸UCC-1 financing statements filed with Delaware SOS for priority security interests
  • ▸Accord & Satisfaction under UCC §3-311 for disputed obligation discharge
  • ▸Tender of Payment protocol under UCC §3-603 for instrument settlement
  • ▸Binding arbitration clauses for dispute resolution outside litigation
  • ▸Registered agent services maintained in Dover, Delaware at all times
